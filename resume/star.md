Given the complexity of the data infrastructure, relying on traditional, manual development cycles meant we were on a direct path to missing the deadline."

**Task (Your Objective)**

> "My objective was to absorb this massive scope change and completely adapt our engineering effort to the new requirements, while still delivering the platform within the original six-week timeframe and guaranteeing our target 99% SLA in production."

**Action (The Execution & Strategic Minimalism)**

> "I applied a framework of Strategic Minimalism. Instead of throwing more manual engineering hours at the problem, I pivoted our execution model. I leveraged a metadata-driven RAG architecture that I had previously designed using Claude, Python, and FastAPI.

Instead of manually rewriting the logic, I fed the new specifications into the RAG system to enforce deterministic pipeline generation. The architecture automatically generated the new Airflow DAGs, created the QA testing protocols, and enforced strict programming standards and documentation. By anchoring the LLM to our metadata, I ensured the generated code was highly predictable, governed, and ready for deployment."

**Result (The Business Impact)**

> "This architecture accelerated our development cycles by 80%. We successfully absorbed the mid-flight requirement changes, generated the necessary codebase deterministically, and met the strict six-week deadline. Most importantly, because the pipelines were generated with built-in QA and standards enforcement, the platform launched seamlessly and successfully sustained a 99% SLA in production."

### **Delivery Tips for the Interview:**

* **Pacing:** Pause briefly after saying *"the business requirements pivoted drastically."* Let the weight of that problem sink in before you introduce your solution.
* **Emphasis:** Hit the words **"Strategic Minimalism"** and **"deterministic pipeline generation"** clearly. These are your strongest technical and leadership hooks.
* **The Follow-Up Hook:** End the result confidently and stop talking. The interviewer will almost certainly lean in to ask exactly how you constrained Claude to achieve that deterministic generation.
